Drone With Robotic Arm

IIT Guwahati Aeromodeling club project

About

techievence march 14, 2020

The main focus of the project was to design and develop a mechanism for robotic arm installed on a drone. The robotic arm was designed with degrees of freedom programmed to accomplish accurately simple light material lifting task to assist in the production line in any industry.

Result

As for the robotic arms, they are internally developed so as to be completely complementary with the airframe. The project weighs grams and it's three-axis arms can carry a maximum payload of about grams and an algorithm works to maintain the drone's center of gravity as it shifts from the movement of the arms.
